Maida – 1½ cups
Curd – ½ cup
Warm water – ¾–1 cup

Baking powder – 1 tsp
Sugar – 1 tsp

Thick curd – ⅓ cup

Lemon juice – 1–2 tbsp

Vinegar – 2 tbsp
Tomato ketchup – 1 tbsp
Sugar – ½ tsp
Salt – ¼ tsp

Simple Recipe

1. Pita: Mix flours, curd, baking powder, sugar, salt, oil, and warm water. Knead, rest 30–40 minutes. Roll and cook on a tawa until they puff.

2. Garlic sauce: Mix mayo, curd, garlic, lemon, salt, pepper.

3. Red sauce: Mix chilli powders, garlic, vinegar, ketchup, sugar, salt. Cook briefly in oil until smooth.

4. Hummus: Blend chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon, salt, olive oil, water.

5. Pickle: Pour hot vinegar-water-salt-sugar mixture over beetroot & carrot.

6. Chicken: Marinate with curd, lemon, oil, ginger-garlic paste, spices. Cook until browned.

7. Paneer: Marinate paneer with curd, spices, ginger-garlic paste. Grill lightly.

8. Falafel: Blend soaked chickpeas with onion, garlic, herbs, spices. Add baking soda, shape, fry.

9. Assemble: Spread hummus + garlic sauce on warm pita. Add chicken or paneer + falafel, salad, pickle, red sauce. Roll and eat.
